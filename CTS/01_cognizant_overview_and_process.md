## 🔄 Typical Interview Process for Experienced Candidates (3+ Years)

Based on recent interview experiences (2024–2025), the process for lateral/experienced hires typically includes:

```
Resume Shortlisting
        ↓
Technical F2F Round (Primary)
  ├── Python Concepts & OOP
  ├── SQL Queries & Theory
  ├── Coding Problems (live)
  └── Project/Resume-Based Discussion
        ↓
HR Round
  ├── Behavioural Questions
  ├── Salary & Relocation Discussion
  └── Cultural Fit Assessment
```

> ⚠️ **For your in-person walk-in:** The technical round is the most critical. Expect 1–2 rounds of technical interviews followed by an HR discussion.

## 💡 Key Tips from Recent Cognizant Interview Experiences

1. **Be honest about your SQL level** — if you claim SQL expertise, expect advanced query questions
2. **Project discussion is important** — know your contribution, tech stack, and impact clearly
3. **Difference between SQL & NoSQL** is frequently asked
4. **Aptitude matters** even in lateral drives — brush up logical reasoning
5. **Basic DSA** (arrays, strings, sorting) can come up in coding rounds
6. **Speak confidently** — communication is evaluated alongside technical skills
